⚠️ What Is Sabotaged Sessions?

Sabotaged Sessions is an ambitious FNF mod created by the CrewCreators team. It’s set in the world of impostors and crewmates — where music, story, and chaos collide. Featuring unique mechanics, intense battles, and deep lore, this mod aims to deliver a complete experience across multiple versions.
